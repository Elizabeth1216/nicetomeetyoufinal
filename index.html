NICE TO MEET YOU

 To understand social anxiety, explore ways to alleviate social anxiety from social situation and manifestation, and
design a product that can ease social anxiety for non-pathological ordinary social phobics.

本项目旨在了解社交恐惧，从社恐情境和表现入手探索缓解社恐的方法，
为非病理性的普通社恐人群设计一款能缓解社恐的产品。

INTRODUCTION

Relationship with our course.

1

Choice

How can designers leverage consumer choice to create optimal experience?
设计师如何利用消费者的选择创造最佳的体验？

According to the actual situation and personal experience, consumers can choose the transparency degree and replacement frequency of the mask independently, so that the mask can provide a personalized "treatment" , thus creating a personalized experience,which is the best. 
消费者根据自己的实际情况和个人使用体验，自主选择面罩的透明程度和调节频率，使面罩起到个性化的“治疗“作用，从而创造个性化的最佳体验。

2

Illusion

We hope to design a product that can block the sight of others and create a sense of security for the user without affecting his/her vision. The user relieves fear and tension by relying on the security brought by the product. The psychological hint and the scene in front of the user can create an illusion that the user can successfully socialize and overcome social fear. In essence, this social smoothness is dependent on our product.
我们希望设计一种产品，既能为使用者阻挡他人的目光，营造一种安全感；又不影响其视觉。使用者依靠产品带来的安全感缓解恐惧与紧张，心理的暗示和眼前的景象能够为使用者创造一种自己能够顺利社交的幻觉，一种克服社恐的幻觉。而实质上，这种社交的顺畅感是依赖我们的产品而产生的。

However, it is not enough to create illusions. We hope that our products can help users gradually remove the barriers of face-to-face communication. Turn the illusion into reality, rather than blindly relying on the illusion and indulging in it. 
但是，仅仅制造幻觉是不够的，我们希望我们的产品能在缓解恐惧感的同时，帮助使用者逐渐消除面对面交流的障碍，让幻觉照进现实，而不是一味地依赖于幻觉，沉溺于幻觉。

RESEARCH PROCESS

We investigated the social situations that social phobia people are generally afraid of and can't be avoided, and the main
manifestations of social phobia people. Then we deduced our design ideas.

我们调查了社恐者普遍害怕且无法避免的社交情境，以及社恐者的主要表现，进而推演出我们的设计构想。

DESIGN CONCEPT


The mask make use of one-way visual effect to create a sense of security for users,
create a good face-to-face communication experience,
and bring progressive psychological treatment for users.

面罩利用单向可视效果为用户创造安全感，带来良好的面对面交流体验，
并起到渐进性心理治疗作用。
